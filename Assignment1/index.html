Six of Crows

By Leigh Bardugo

Chapter 1

Our story begins by introducing a primary plot element: a mysterious drug called "jurda". In the dark of the night, as the house of a rich merchant, the hired guards are called to witness a demonstration of the drug on a magic user, known as a Grisha, with disasterous results.

Chapter 2

In chapter 2, we are introduced to some of our main characters, the Crows, as they go up against a rival gang. Told from the perspective of Inej, the Wraith, a knife-wielding thief in the employ of the infamous Crows gang. The Crows include Inej, sharpshooter Jesper, and their leader, the mysterious glove-wearing, cane-using criminal, Kaz Brekker.

>

Chapter 3

Chapter 3 is told from the persective of Kaz Brekker, victorious from their recent bout with the rival gang, the Black Tips. Here, we get some backstory and insight into the leader of the Crows.
