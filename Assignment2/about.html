About Six of Crows

Six of Crows is a fantasy heist novel by Leigh Bardugo, and the first book in the Six of Crows duology. In it, a band of questionably moral characters from diverse background come together to infiltrate an impenetrable prison and rescue a scientist responsible for a powerful new drug that spells danger for their world.
